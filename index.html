Who Am I?

By nature, curiosity guides my feet, yet, a cautious mind can trip me in my path. In nature, however, fierce passions compels me to take the leap and survive; for I am light peaking through the cracks in a dark place, shining just enough to see the wonders hidden beneath.

Blank canvases are merely peeks into vast wells of sublime imaginations. Such as is art, the creative pen meets the sword of the mind, sculpting words into a vision, taken apart only by what is taken in heart.

What have I done?

Roadie

Roadie is the all-in-one booking and event management platform, allowing both artists and clients to expand their network and achieve new career heights. Founded in a universal love for music and the arts, this Capstone project sets the tone for hassle-free communications and direct collaboration. Using React/Redux Toolkit for framework and Bootstrap for design.

Live Site GitHub Repo
#NowThatsCap

Inspired by Black city culture, #NowThatsCap is a trivia app that tests the user's knowledge of things they already know and things they need to know but may not regarding anything and everything Black including history, culture, music, food and art. Audience Choice Winner of Pursuit's 2021 Just Build Hackathon.

Live Site GitHub Repo
Bloop... Coming Soon!

Bloop is an app to share with friends the behind-the-scenes, candid moments, and funny shots of life in short video format. Users will be able to upload videos and create a post, comment their thoughts on videos and see a feed of other user's posts. Inspired by the trending TikTok app, built with React, Express.js, Node.js, and Firebase.
